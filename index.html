    Publications


    		 BVINet: Unlocking Blind Video Inpainting with Zero Annotations
    Zhiliang Wu, Kerui Chen, Kun Li, Hehe Fan, Yi Yang
    Paper / Code
    ICCV 2025

    We introduces a new task called blind video inpainting and proposes an end-to-end network, BVINet, which simultaneously learns to locate and inpaint corrupted regions without requiring annotated masks, enabling fully automatic video restoration.



    		Prompt-Aware Controllable Shadow Removal
    Kerui Chen, Zhiliang Wu, Wenjin Hou, Kun Li, Hehe Fan, Yi Yang,
    Paper / Code
    IJCAI 2025

    We proposes PACSRNet, a prompt-aware controllable shadow removal network that enables user-guided, annotation-free shadow removal based on simple prompts, addressing limitations of existing methods in control and flexibility.



    		Brant-X: A unified physiological signal alignment framework
    Daoze Zhang, Zhizhang Yuan, Junru Chen, Kerui Chen, Yang Yang
    Paper / Code
    KDD 2024

    We introduce Brant-X, a framework that transfers EEG knowledge to other physiological signals for better cross-signal understanding.
